Welcome to Celtec Games

Celtec is a small independent games company developing original titles for desktop, mobile, and console platforms. Driven by a passion for creativity and innovation, Celtec combines artistic talent with cutting-edge technology to produce games that both educate and entertain players of all ages. From immersive storytelling to engaging gameplay mechanics, every project is crafted with care, ensuring a memorable experience for children and adults alike.

Based in North Wales, UK, Celtec has grown from a small creative idea into a multi-platform development studio. Our team has experience building games for mobile devices (iOS and Android), desktop systems, and major consoles including PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch. This cross-platform approach allows us to reach a wide audience and bring our unique ideas to players wherever they are.

At Celtec, we believe that great games are built on more than just code they are built on people. Ask anyone on our team what makes Celtec special, and they will tell you it is the shared sense of creativity, vision, and belonging. We foster an environment where ideas are encouraged, talent is nurtured, and everyone plays a part in shaping the final product.
