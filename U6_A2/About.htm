About Us

Celtec is a small independent games company based in North East Wales, UK. We combine talent and passion to create games that educate and entertain children and adults alike.

We develop games across multiple platforms including iOS, Android, PlayStation, Xbox and Nintendo Switch.

At Celtec, our secret is simple — creativity, vision, technology and a sense of belonging. We got to where we are today by raising the game, empowering our people and fuelling our passion to make the greatest cross-platform games in the country!
